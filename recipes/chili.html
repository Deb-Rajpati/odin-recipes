Chili

Description

Chili is a spicy stew made with ground meat, beans, tomatoes, and a blend of flavorful spices. It's a comforting dish perfect for cold days and gatherings.

This recipe is easy to customize and delivers a hearty, satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in a large pot.
  2. Add onion and garlic; cook until softened.
  3. Stir in tomatoes, beans, chili powder, and cumin.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve hot, topped with cheese or sour cream if desired.
