Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a breakfast favorite, known for their fluffy texture and golden color. They are quick to make and can be served with syrup, fruit, or your favorite toppings.

This simple recipe yields light and airy pancakes perfect for any morning.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients; stir until just combined.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and grease lightly.
  5. Pour batter onto skillet and cook until bubbles form.
  6. Flip and cook until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.
