Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's hearty, comforting, and perfect for family gatherings.

This recipe is easy to follow and results in a delicious, cheesy lasagna that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown ground beef in a skillet, then add marinara sauce and simmer.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat layers and top with Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  8. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
